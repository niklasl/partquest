Epilogue

You stand at the door, staring up at the giant in the expensive suit.

“Uh, the “Neutral One” wants to see me.”

The bouncer looks skeptical, but he leads you inside. The boss himself is sitting in the dark (he forbids the use of electrical lighting during the day time), folding paper cranes out of candy wrappers. He looks up. Seeing that it is you, he crushes a finished crane in his fist.

“Uh, sir. Mr. Gore. I’ve done what you’ve asked. My quest is complete.”

You pull out... A perfect, ruby tomato.

“Sir, Dino’s tomatoes are by far the worse part of his caponata. They’re his secret ingredient. So important are they to his recipe, he flies them over from... Italy.”

The gasp is audible throughout the room. Even the guards look distressed. The Neutral One holds his hand out for the evidence. Putting on his reading glasses, he studies it carefully before nodding slowly.

“Thank you. My mind is at rest, and your carbon karma is nearly restored.”
