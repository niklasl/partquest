Boston, 2020

“Papa” Al Gore leans over you menacingly.

“I know it was you that brought New Zealand kumquats into town. Our CO2 footprint is catastrophic! You’re a bad seed, smuggling imported fruits into town. I oughta cut your nose off!”

“No, Al!” You cry. “I’ll do anything!”

He ponders for a moment. “You could do me a little favour. There’s this caponata that I love at Dino’s Delectable Delecatessian Den. It’s a weakness. I hear the ingredients are mostly from around the US, but there must be a couple ingredients that are imported. Dino won’t tell me! There just has to be. I need you to find out for me. What is in Dino’s magical caponata? Where do the ingredients come from? And what,” he says dramatically, “is the carbon footprint of my precious caponata?! Find me the ingredient with the worst carbon footprint and i can replace it to build my own eco-caponata, and sleep the sleep of carbon-innocence again!”
